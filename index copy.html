Welcome to MARC

Multidisciplinary AI Research Centre

Multidisciplinary AI Research Centre (MARC) of University of Peradeniya, is a research platform dedicated for bridging the gap between Sri Lanka's today's reality with tomorrow's potential through pioneering innovative solutions with Cutting-Edge Tech. By unlocking the potentials of AI, core mission of MARC is to address economical and societal challenges of Sri Lanka by developing AI-powered solutions for pressing issues like education, healthcare, climate change, agriculture, and management. MARC stands at the forefront of Sri Lanka's journey into the Fourth Industrial Revolution (IR4.0) and beyond.
