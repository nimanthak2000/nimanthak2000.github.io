About Us

Multidisciplinary AI Research Centre - MARC

The Multidisciplinary AI Research Centre (MARC) at the University of Peradeniya is a pioneering initiative at the forefront of Sri Lanka's technological advancement. We bring together experts from various fields including Engineering, Science, Agriculture, Medicine, and Humanities to drive innovative research in Artificial Intelligence (AI). MARC aims to harness the transformative potential of AI to address pressing societal challenges and drive sustainable development in Sri Lanka and beyond.

Mission and Vision

Mission of MARC

Our mission at MARC is to leverage AI research and innovation to drive sustainable development and societal progress in Sri Lanka. We are committed to developing cutting-edge AI solutions that address real-world challenges and contribute to a brighter future for all.



Vision of MARC

At MARC, we envision a future where AI-powered technologies play a central role in shaping a more inclusive, equitable, and sustainable society. We strive to be leaders in AI research and education, driving positive change and innovation on a global scale.

More Details

Establishment

The Multidisciplinary AI Research Centre (MARC) was established at the University of Peradeniya with a visionary goal: to position Sri Lanka at the forefront of the Fourth Industrial Revolution (IR4.0) and prepare for the impending IR5.0. With the university's rich expertise spanning Engineering, Science, Agriculture, Medicine, Humanities, and Management, MARC was conceived as a bold initiative to capitalize on the transformative potential of Artificial Intelligence (AI) for national development.



University's Role and Commitment

The University of Peradeniya plays a pivotal role in driving AI research in Sri Lanka, committing to fostering innovation and societal impact. Through MARC, the university demonstrates its dedication to leveraging its diverse academic strengths for national development. With a focus on collaborative research, capacity building, and outreach, the university aims to position Sri Lanka as a leader in AI innovation, equipped with the innovative minds and skilled workforce needed for a future powered by AI.
