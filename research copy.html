Overview of the Area Covered by MARC

The Multidisciplinary AI Research Centre (MARC) covers a wide range of research areas, leveraging its diverse expertise to address pressing challenges in Sri Lanka and beyond. MARC focuses on utilizing Artificial Intelligence (AI) to drive innovation and positive change in various sectors including education, healthcare, climate change, agriculture, and management.



Descriptions of Each Research Area

1. AI in Education


 Develop AI-powered learning tools, personalize education, and bridge the digital divide.
 Implementation of virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) in educational settings.


2. AI in Healthcare


 Development of AI algorithms for early detection of diseases such as cancer and diabetes.
 Implementation of AI-driven decision support systems for healthcare providers.


3. AI in Climate Change


 Development of AI models for predicting natural disasters like floods and droughts.
  Develop AI-driven solutions for climate prediction,and sustainable practices.


4. AI in Agriculture/Food Science


 Development of AI-driven precision farming techniques for optimizing irrigation and fertilizer use.
 Implementation of AI-based systems for detecting food contamination and ensuring food safety.


5. AI in Management


 Implement AI for business process automation, supply-chain management, decision-making support, and market analysis.
 Implementation of AI-driven chatbots for customer service and support.
